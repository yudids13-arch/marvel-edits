Emotional Edit

Slow build with quiet opening, rising tension through mid-section, emotional peak at 70%, resolution and exhale close. Soft slow-mo on key reaction shots. Music cues tied to facial expressions.

  • Cut Speed 3–8s early, 1–2s at peak
  • Effects 0.5× slow-mo, soft zoom-in, light contrast lift
